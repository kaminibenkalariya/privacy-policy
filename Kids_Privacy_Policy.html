Overview

We created this Privacy Policy to explain clearly what information we collect, how we use it, and when it may be shared. This policy applies to all our mobile applications and games.

Our children's apps are designed to comply with the Google Play Families Policy and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our apps, limited technical information may be collected such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Language settings
  • Anonymous usage data
  • IP address (for security and functionality)

This information is used only to operate, maintain and improve our apps. We do not use this information to personally identify users.

2. Advertising

Our apps may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). For children’s apps:

  • Only contextual, family‑safe ads are shown.
  • No personalized advertising is used.
  • No Advertising ID tracking or profiling of children is performed.
  • Ads are filtered for age‑appropriate content.

3. In‑App Purchases

Some apps may offer optional in‑app purchases. We do not collect or store billing information. Payments are handled by Google Play or the platform payment provider.

4. Children's Privacy

We respect children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our children's apps show only contextual ads and comply with COPPA requirements.

5. Saving Drawings or Media

Some apps allow saving drawings or images locally to your device gallery. These files remain on your device and are not uploaded or shared by us.

6. Support Information

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and any information you provide. This is used only to respond to your request.

Cookies (Website Only)

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. Mobile apps generally do not use cookies.
